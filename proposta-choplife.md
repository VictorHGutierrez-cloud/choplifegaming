## 02 — Context

Choplife Gaming operates in the gaming & betting sector in Central Africa. HR runs performance reviews in an existing flat-fee HR platform, but once reviews finish, there is no structured path from identified gaps to training. Mandatory compliance trainings (AML, responsible gaming, gambling) are delivered via PDFs and an external partner — difficult to track at scale.

Mireille Uwara's priority: *"After the performance reviews I think we have all the gaps mapped out — then how do we link that to trainings?"*

The approach is **integration, not replacement** — Factorial adds Performance + LMS alongside the current HR stack.

## 05 — Solution modules

### Performance Reviews
- Self-assessment, manager review, 360° peer feedback, project-based reviews
- Custom templates, automated reminders, structured workflows
- Score distribution, strengths, areas to improve, action plans

### Talent Management — 9-box grid
- Map performance vs potential
- Identify rising stars, flight risks, succession candidates

### Performance → Learning
- Request course from action plan after review
- Pick from catalog or propose external course (provider, cost, dates)
- HR approval workflow

### Training Management
- AI course creation from PDF/Word uploads
- Assign to individuals or teams; training groups
- Mandatory compliance with expiration tracking
- Mobile completion for employees
- Virtual sessions with external links tracked
- Training budgets: direct + indirect costs in real time
